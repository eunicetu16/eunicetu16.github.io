Node-link diagram with force-directed layout

The layout visually represents network data by simulating forces between nodes: repulsive forces push nodes apart to prevent overlap, while attractive forces pull connected nodes closer together, resulting in an optimized layout that reveals patterns and relationships within the network.

Adjacency matrix

It representation of a network organizes nodes and their relationships into a matrix format, where rows and columns represent nodes, and the matrix cells indicate the presence or absence of connections between nodes.

Black hat and White hat visualizations

White hat visualization emphasizes presenting data objectively and accurately, prioritizing transparency and clarity in communication. In contrast, black hat visualization involves critically examining visualizations to identify potential biases, limitations, and inaccuracies, ensuring the reliability and integrity of the presented information.

Black Hat

The graph from CNN depicting the number of Democrats, Republicans, and Independent voter who belived that Terry Schiabo should have her feeding tube removed in 2005. This court agreement by political party by percentage is misleading, as it appears to show three times as much support from Democrats compared to Republicans. However, upon closer examination, it's evident that the vertical axis scale starts at 50%, masking the slight 8% difference where slightly more Democrats supported the decision than Republicans.

White Hat

The redesign graph has helpful title and labels that describe and contextualize the visualization. The baseline from zero on the y-axis to show that threr isn't much difference between Democratic and Republican opnion.
